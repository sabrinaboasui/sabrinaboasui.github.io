Zhixian SUI

Senior Lecturer at University of New South Wales, Australia
Email: zhixian.sui@unsw.edu.au
Address: School of Public Health, University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia


I received my PhD from the University of Adelaide where I carried out my research on early origins of obesity in pregnant women who were overweight or obese. After graduation, I continued my career at the University of Sydney. During the time, I worked on nutritional ecology and secondary analysis of national surveys. At the moment, I am a full-time senior lecturer at the University of New South Wales, teaching leadership and policy changes in school of public health. As part of my industry collaboration, I am also the clinical research coordinator with BMI Clinic in Double Bay.

My supervision areas includes Obesity, Bariatric Medicine, Leadership in Health Sector, Research Method, Statistical Analysis.


Recent Activities:
  • 01/12/2018: I received CASS Foundation Travel Award to present my work in Digestive Disease Week in San Diego, USA.
  • 26/09/2018: I presented my work at International Federation for the Surgery of Obesity and Metabolic Disorders in Dubai, UAE.
  • 05/06/2018: I coordinated the academic visiting and training events from Shanghai JiaoTong University and TongJi University at UNSW.
